Find a Therapist

Explore these trusted platforms to connect with licensed therapists:

  • BetterHelp – Affordable online therapy with licensed professionals.
  • Talkspace – Online therapy for individuals, couples, and teens.
  • Therapy for Black Girls – Focused on mental health for Black women and girls.
  • Psychology Today – Search for therapists in your local area.
  • Mentally Aware Nigeria Initiative – Mental health support and counseling in Nigeria.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where can I locate the nearest therapist?

You can find the nearest therapists through platforms like Psychology Today or Mentally Aware Nigeria Initiative.

2. What causes depression or anxiety?

Depression and anxiety can stem from various factors, including stress, trauma, genetics, and chemical imbalances in the brain. Seeking professional help is important in addressing these issues.

3. How do I deal with family problems affecting my mental health?

Family issues can have a significant impact on your mental well-being. Talking to a therapist or counselor can help provide strategies for coping with these challenges. You can also seek family therapy to address underlying issues together.
