Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where can I locate the nearest therapy center?

Use platforms like Psychology Today or Mentally Aware Nigeria Initiative to find therapy centers near you. Many are also available online.

2. What are the common causes of depression and anxiety?

Depression and anxiety can result from genetic, biological, environmental, or psychological factors. Common triggers include stress, trauma, loneliness, or medical conditions.

3. How can I deal with family-related mental health issues?

It’s important to communicate openly and seek professional help if needed. Therapy can help mediate conflicts and provide coping strategies for dealing with family-related stress.

4. How can I identify if I need professional mental health support?

Look for signs like persistent sadness, overwhelming anxiety, changes in behavior, or thoughts of self-harm. If these feelings persist, seek help from a licensed therapist or counselor.

5. Are there free or affordable therapy options in Nigeria?

Yes, organizations like Mentally Aware Nigeria Initiative provide free or low-cost mental health support.
