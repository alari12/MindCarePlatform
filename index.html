Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find a therapist near me?

You can find a list of verified therapists on trusted platforms such as Psychology Today.

What are common causes of depression?

Depression can be caused by various factors, including genetics, trauma, stress, and chemical imbalances in the brain.

How can I handle family problems?

Effective communication, seeking professional counseling, and fostering understanding can help address family issues.
