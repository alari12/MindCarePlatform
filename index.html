Find a Therapist

Explore these trusted platforms to connect with licensed therapists:

  • BetterHelp – Affordable online therapy with licensed professionals.
  • Talkspace – Online therapy for individuals, couples, and teens.
  • Therapy for Black Girls – Focused on mental health for Black women and girls.
  • Psychology Today – Search for therapists in your local area.
  • Mentally Aware Nigeria Initiative – Mental health support and counseling in Nigeria.

Meet the Creator

Hi, I’m Alari, the creator of MindCare Hub. As someone passionate about mental health, I created this platform to provide a safe space for individuals seeking support and awareness. I believe everyone deserves access to mental health resources, and my mission is to break the stigma around mental health in Nigeria and beyond.

Through this site, I hope to inspire hope and encourage people to take the first step towards a healthier and happier life.
